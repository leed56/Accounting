## Full description

BizManager helps Sri Lankan small business owners manage daily operations from phone or web.

**Finance**
- Record income and expenses in seconds
- Track money to receive (customers) and money to pay (suppliers)
- Attach receipts; approve large payments

**Staff**
- Mark attendance, manage leave
- Generate monthly payroll with EPF deductions
- Download payslips and share via WhatsApp

**Owner dashboard**
- Today's income, expenses, and profit at a glance
- Cash and bank balance
- AI daily briefing in English, Sinhala, or Tamil

**Built for Sri Lanka**
- LKR currency formatting
- Local payment methods (cash, bank, LankaQR, cheque)
- Configurable VAT and SSCL settings

Free trial · Small Office plan (up to 3 users)
